Fisher Evidence Reporting App

The Fisher is a member of the weasel family. After being extirpated from Washington State in the 1900's, Fishers are now being re-introduced to the Olympic and Cascade mountain ranges in Washington State.

In order to keep track of where the Fishers are located and where they are expanding to, users can use the map below to report signs of Fisher presence (tracks, scat, fur, etc.) to help conservationists track where the Fishers have spread to.

Use the point tool to record the location of signs such as scat and fur, use the line tool to record signs such as tracks over a distance, and use the shape tool to plot a combination of signs on the map. Use these sources below to help identify Fisher tracks, scat, and other potential signs:
